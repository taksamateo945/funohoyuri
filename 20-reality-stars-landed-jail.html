20 Reality Stars That Have Landed in Jail

Posted by Martina Birk on Saturday, March 23, 2024

Living life in the spotlight can be extremely difficult for anyone.  However, most celebrities don’t actually want to be famous, they just want to work – and the fame is just something they learn to deal with.  Reality TV stars on the other hand, have signed up to be followed around by camera crews and have their ever move caught on tape.  You’d think these people would behave a little more responsibly as a result, but as many reality stars have shown, they’re going to live however they want to regardless of the cameras.  As a result, almost all of their bad decisions have been documented and published on websites and blogs all across the internet.  Here are 20 reality stars that have landed in jail.

 (Photo by Brunswick County Sheriff’s Department via Getty Images)

Khadijah Rowe

Star of Style Networks’ now defunct series, Big Rich Atlanta, Khadijah Rowe spent a few nights in jail in 2013 after being arrested on charges of felony battery.

(image via http://realarticulate.tumblr.com)

Sky

During this past season of VH1’s Black Ink Crew, Black Ink staff member, Sky, spent a decent amount of time in a jail. Apparently, some of Sky’s old bad behavior finally caught up with her and there was nowhere left to run.  Despite her circumstances, her co-workers/cast members were very supportive of her and stood by her throughout the entire ordeal.

(image via https://twitter.com/CocaineLike80s)

Nick Hogan

In 2007, Nick Hogan was involved in a very serious car crash in which the passenger of his car was left with injuries that have forced him to live his life in a nursing home.  The crash that left Hogan with several legal charges, and in 2008, he was arrested and charged.  Hogan was eventually sentenced to 8 months, and he was released in the fall of 2008.

 (Photo by Joseph Garnett, Jr.-Pool/Getty Images)

Mendeecees Harris

Love & Hip Hop’s Mendeecees Harris just can’t seem to stay out of trouble.  Last year, he was arrested, jailed, and eventually put in prison – leaving his fiancé, Yandy Smith to hold down the fort at home.  According to recent reports, there’s a very good chance Harris will end up behind bars again after pleading guilty to drug trafficking.

(image via https://twitter.com/BET)

Alexis Neiers

Once a member of the cast of the short-lived reality series, Pretty Wild, Alexis Neiers certainly isn’t as innocent as she looks.  Neiers, a convicted felon, was involved in the “Bling Ring” burglaries which made headlines when it was revealed that a group of teenagers and young adults had been robbing the homes of celebrities in California.

(image via https://twitter.com/Lawson_Kincaid/)

Renee Alway

In the summer 2013, former America’s Next Top Model contestant, Renee Alway, was arrested in California. Alway’s arrest included a number of charges, and she had actually been arrested several times earlier that year.  After less than six months, Alway was sentenced to 12 years in prison.

(image via https://www.pinterest.com/cbcrawford32/)

Kim Richards

Real housewife of Beverly Hills, Kim Richards may look like she has it all together, but her actions have suggested otherwise. Recently, she was arrested and charged with trespassing, public intoxication, resisting an officer, and battery on a police officer in Beverly Hills.  After spending a night in jail, Richards’ bond was paid and she was sent home.

(Photo by Frederick M. Brown/Getty Images)

Jason Wahler

Former Laguna Beach star, Jason Wahler, seemed like the type of kid who had it all. However, he’s still managed to get himself into some trouble over the years. In 2007, Wahler spent some time in jail after being involved in a physical altercation in Seattle. In 2009, Wahler was again arrested in Seattle.  According to a source: “He will serve 120 days in jail but could get out after 80 days for good behavior.”

(Photo by Astrid Stawiarz/Getty Images)

Flavor Flav

One generation knows Flavor Flav as part of the iconic hip-hop group, Public Enemy.  Another knows him as the guy with the crazy clocks from VH1’s Flavor of Love. Although Flav’s fun-loving personality has made him a winner with fans.  His behavior hasn’t always made him a winner with the law.  Throughout the years, Flav has been in jail numerous times dating back to his teenage years.

 (Photo by Ethan Miller/Getty Images)

Apollo Nida

Apollo Nida is another reality TV star who just can’t seem to stay out of trouble.  This real husband of Atlanta is known for serving some real time, in fact, he’s actually in prison right now.  Although his wife, Phaedra Parks, has always been his biggest advocate, it seems like even she has had enough of his antics this time.

(Photo by Rick Diamond/Getty Images for Cirque du Soleil)

Nicole ‘Snooki’ Polizzi

Jersey Shore star, Nicole ‘ Snooki’ Polizzi, was arrested in 2010 for disorderly conduct while filming for the show.  Although she only spend one night in jail, her fans were waiting for her when she exited the building.

 (Photo by Robin Marchant/Getty Images)

Nicole Richie

In 2007, Nicole Richie was sentenced to serve four days in jail after a DUI charge.  However, Richie only ended up serving 82 minutes of that sentence.  Talk about cutting it short! According to sources, Richie was “treated in the same manner as other inmates with a similar sentence,” but most people don’t seem to be quite that lucky.

(Photo by Jason Merritt/Getty Images)

Khloe Kardashian

Similar to Nicole Richie, Khloe Kardashian didn’t spend much time in jail. Also charged with a DUI, Kardashian was sentenced to serve 30 days in jail, but only ended up serving three hours.

 (Photo by Frederick M. Brown/Getty Images)

Paris Hilton

Socialite turned reality TV star turned business woman, Paris Hilton, has experienced a lot of growth over the years.  However, in 2007, she was still up to her hold tricks. During that year, she was arrested after violating an agreement she made not to drive with her suspended license.  As a result, she was sentenced to serve 45 days in jail.

(Photo by Mike Windle/Getty Images for Warner Music Group)

T.I.

Thanks to his realty show, the world has now seen a much softer side of rapper, T.I.  However, there’s also another side to the trouble man.  T.I. has served time in both the county jail and the federal prison on probation violations and gun charges respectively.

(Photo by Rick Diamond/Getty Images for PUMA)

Richard Hatch

First ever Survivor winner, Richard Hatch, was convicted of tax evasion in 2006.  After serving a three-year sentence, Hatch was required to serve a nine month sentence. However, over the years, Hatch has maintained his innocence.

(Photo by Stephen Chernin/Getty Images)

Mark Wahlberg

Award winning actor turned reality TV star, Mark Wahlberg may seem like a clean-cut guy now, but back in the day Marky Mark was a real life bad boy. At just 16 years-old, Wahlberg served a 45 day jail sentence after pleading guilty to assault.

(Photo by Ryan Pierse/Getty Images for Paramount Pictures International)

Amber Portwood

Teen mom, Amber Portwood, has had a pretty rough go of things.  After her terrible relationship and subsequent break up with baby daddy, Gary Shirley, Amber was sentenced to jail time after being arrested on drug charges.  Although most people are ashamed of their criminal history, Amber has been very open about her experiences.

(Photo by Rich Polk/Getty Images for MTV)

Teresa Giudice

Real Housewives of New Jersey star, Teresa Giudice, made headlines everywhere when she was sentenced to serve 15 months behind bars for pleading guilty to fraud which she is currently serving. Although she has been away from the camera, Giudice is still very much in the spotlight

 (Photo by Mike Coppola/Getty Images)

Jenelle Evans

Jenelle Evans always seems to be up to no good.  Her parenting skills and morals have constantly been questioned, and she doesn’t seem to care.  Since becoming a reality TV star, Evans has been arrested and jailed several times for crimes such as stalking, drug possession, and harassment – just to name a few.

(Photo by Brunswick County Sheriff’s Office via Getty Images)
