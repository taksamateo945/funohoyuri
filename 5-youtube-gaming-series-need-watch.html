5 YouTube Gaming Series You Need To Watch

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Saturday, June 15, 2024

YouTube has a very active gaming community. However, there’s so much to choose from that it can be difficult to find out what to watch. You’ve come to the right place, as we’re here to help.

We’ve compiled a list of 5 YouTube gaming series for you to watch. There’s a range of games included, such as The Last Guardian, Until Dawn and Crash Bandicoot, so there’ll be something for everyone!

1. Jacksepticeye – The Last Guardian

Jacksepticeye’s playthrough of The Last Guardian is one you do not want to miss out on. The game allows the player to control a young boy who has to co-operate and perform tasks with a half-bird-half-mammal creature named Trico. Watching Jacksepticeye play The Last Guardian is incredibly emotional; it’s a journey, filled with beautiful scenes and a phenomenal soundtrack to accompany the gameplay.

2. UberHaxorNova – Crash Bandicoot N. Sane Trilogy

Join UberHaxorNova, otherwise known as James, delve into a blast from the past as he plays Crash Bandicoot! This game has heavy nostalgic vibes since it’s a remastered version of the original game from the 1990s. The Crash Bandicoot N. Sane Trilogy is a collection of the first three games in the series; Crash Bandicoot, Cortex Strikes Back and Warped. If you’ve already played Crash Bandicoot, you will most likely relate incredibly to James’ gameplay!

3. Achievement Hunter on Let’s Play – Until Dawn

Achievement Hunter’s let’s play of the award winning game, Until Dawn, is something you do not want to miss out on. Until Dawn is an interactive survival horror game. It utilises the butterfly effect, where every action made can lead to an array of different outcomes, meaning hundreds of different scenarios are possible. The player switches control between eight different characters, where choosing between options can be critical. We don’t want to spoil the guys of Achievement Hunter’s gameplay, but the ending is one you need to see!

4. CrankGameplays – Dream Daddy

Dream Daddy: A Dad Dating Simulator is a visual novel video game, published and developed by the Game Grumps. It’s an interactive game where you play as a dad and can choose between different fathers to date. The player is able to customise their character, making the dad truly unique. Join CrankGameplays, otherwise known as Ethan, on this dad dating journey and find out what route he takes!

5. Markiplier – Little Nightmares

Dive in and unravel the secrets of Little Nightmares in this quite short, yet creepy, game with Markiplier! Little Nightmares is a puzzle-platformer horror game. You play as a character named Six who is tiny in comparison to her surroundings and other creatures in the game.

Which YouTube gaming series is your favourite? Or do you have one we haven’t included? Let us know your thoughts on Twitter or Facebook!
