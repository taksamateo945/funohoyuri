Who is Cherrelle:

Cherrelle is a famous R&B Singer. She was born on October 13, 1958 and her birthplace is Los Angeles, CA. Cherrelle is also well known as, R&B vocalist best known for solo tracks like 1988’s “Everything I Miss at Home”. She is also famous for her many collaborations with Alexander O’Neal, such as “Saturday Love”.

Cherrelle is originated from United States. Her song “I Didn’t Mean to Turn You On” was covered by Mariah Carey on the pop superstar’s 2001 album “Glitter”.
