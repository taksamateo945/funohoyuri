Chris Brown, French Montana Get Punk'd By a 9-Year-Old Girl: Watch Now

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Friday, February 9, 2024

Now that Chris Brown is a dad himself, the producers at BET’s reboot of Punk’d decided to play a little trick on the R&B star. In a new sneak peek from the show’s series premiere, the singer is approached on the set of his music video by a 9-year-old girl named Jayla. 

After she offers him some dance tips, Brown, 26, and his rapper pal French Montana start asking the little cutie about her parents.  

“I lost her, I don’t know where she is,” a nervous-looking Jayla tells the musicians of her mom.   

“Did you run away?” Brown asks.
