Chris Pratt French Braids Intern's Hair During Interview

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Sunday, March 3, 2024

Ok, this is amazing. Chris Pratt stopped during the middle of an interview with Entertainment Tonight to reveal he can seriously french braid.

The hunky actor, who appeared on ET to promote his new film Guardians of the Galaxy, was challenged to french braid an intern’s hair after he posted an image of his wife Anna Faris‘ hair in a braid with the caption: “This is a weird thing to brag about but I did that glorious french braid. #Baller #man #ManBraid #RealMenBraid #isItBrade? #SpellingQuestion #StillBallerTho #WhyIsItFrench??? #ICallItAFreedomBraid #GoUsa.”

After admitting that his Guardians of the Galaxy workout involved “a lot of crying” and that Faris “likes me better fat,” the Parks and Recreation actor, 35, took on the challenge with zero hesitation and even complimented the intern, Jen, on her lovely hair.

After completing the task—and after making us swoon—the funny man then proceeded to be even more adorable, telling the reporter, “You wouldn’t want to use a rubber band [to secure the braid]. That could damage the hair—you have to use something called a scrunchie.”
