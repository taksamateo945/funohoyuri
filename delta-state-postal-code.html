Delta State Postal Code

Posted by Martina Birk on Sunday, February 11, 2024

Delta State postal codes are a series of unique 6 digits (320001 to 334196) that are used for sorting mail. Each of these digits has its significance; the second and third digits are a representation of the dispatch district, the fourth digit is a representation of the route, and the last two digits represent the delivery location. 

Therefore, knowing the right postal code equals ease in getting a post delivered to a particular destination. Generally, Nigeria’s postal codes are divided according to regions and Delta State falls under the South-South geopolitical zone. The postal codes in Delta come differently, depending on the local government area, district, and even area.

This means that Delta State postal codes are in different categories. In this article, we have categorized these codes under different local governments, districts, and areas for easy understanding. Here is all to know about Delta State postal codes.

Delta State Local Governments and Postal Codes

There are 25 local governments in Delta State. Each of these local government have districts under them and in turn, there are different postal codes that represent each district and area.

The postcode for the state ranges from 320001 to 334196 and no two local governments share the same code. These local governments are as follows:

Delta State Zip Code

There are numerous unique postal codes in Delta State. These numbers differ depending on the villages, districts, and regions. Despite the differences in the numbers, one unique thing about these postal codes is that they range from 320001 to 334196.

These codes are under NIPOST and the headquarters postal code. While this is important to note, knowing the postal code designated to your exact location is more important. For instance, you cannot use the code of Ugheli for Asaba and vice versa.

Entry of a wrong postal code automatically means that in the case of delivery, your package may be dropped at the wrong location. This being said, here are all the postcodes in Delta State. They are segmented into local governments and other localities.
