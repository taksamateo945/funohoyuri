Doutzen Kroes Breastfeeds Baby Daughter Inside Vogue Netherlands: Pics

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Monday, March 11, 2024

Would you look at these beauts!

Doutzen Kroes may be a supermodel, but she’s also a woman with a gorgeous family—including two children with her husband, celeb DJ Sunnery James—and she’s showing them off inside the March 2015 issue of Vogue Netherlands.

To celebrate her 30th birthday, the Dutch-born Victoria’s Secret Angel posed around the world in New York, Los Angeles, and Amsterdam for three collector’s covers and an inside spread that feature the summer style trends and her loves: James, 35, Phyllon, 4, and Myllena, who was born last July.

The photos are mostly black and white, but one sweet color shot features the family snuggled up in bed, with Kroes breastfeeding her little girl while father and son rub noses.
