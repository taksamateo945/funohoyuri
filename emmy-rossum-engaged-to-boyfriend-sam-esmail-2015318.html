Emmy Rossum Engaged to Boyfriend Sam Esmail

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Thursday, January 25, 2024

Bling bling! Actress Emmy Rossum is engaged to her write-director boyfriend, Sam Esmail, her rep confirms to Us Weekly. The Shameless star, 28, and Mr. Robot creator, 37, who have been dating for two years, got engaged over the weekend in Los Angeles.

The couple sparked engagement rumors last December after the star stepped out in New York City with a diamond ring. 

The now-engaged pair made their debut as a couple in June 2014 at the premiere of Esmail’s flick Cornet, in which Rossum starred.
