Posted by Martina Birk on Thursday, March 21, 2024

Kandi Burruss had a good day, and she has her entire family to thank for it. The Xscape singer and businesswoman took to Instagram, where she shared a few personal photos from a special family gathering for Father’s Day.

The pictures confirmed that the Real Housewives of Atlanta star was surrounded by love as she and husband Todd Tucker, marked the special day.

Kandi’s daughter, Riley Burruss, 17, and her stepdaughter, Kaela Tucker, 24, were present along with her mother, Mama Joyce.

The diva’s son, Ace, 4, daughter Blaze, seven months, and her dad, Dr. Titus Burruss, and stepmom Brenda Burruss also participated in the festivities.

She penned this message: “Happy Father’s Day to my dad @drtpburrussjr & Happy Heavenly Father’s Day to my stepfather #LeroyJones! I hope all Fathers are enjoying your day today! ❤️”
