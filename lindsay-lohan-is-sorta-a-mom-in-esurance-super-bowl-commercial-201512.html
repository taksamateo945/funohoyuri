Lindsay Lohan Is &quot;Sorta&quot; a Mom in Esurance Super Bowl Commercial

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Sunday, April 14, 2024

Lindsay Lohan is “sorta” a mom in Esurance’s Super Bowl XLIX ad. The Parent Trap alum, who was arrested for DUI in May 2007 and another that July, rolls up in a minivan and attempts to pick up two kids in the funny ad, which promotes the insurance company’s CoverageMyWay plan.

“Your mom is a terrible drive,” the boy tells his friend while Lohan pulls up in a hot mess of a van, while almost taking out the school bus. “Get in!” she then yells at the kids, who respond, “That’s not my mom.”
