Astrology Birth Chart of Nick Cannon (TV Actor) 2024

Posted by Martina Birk on Sunday, July 7, 2024

Nicholas Scott Cannon is an American comedian, rapper, television presenter and actor. On television, Cannon started as a teenager on All That before performing The Nick Cannon Show, Wild 'n Out, America's Got Talent, Lip Sync Battle Shorties, and The Masked Singer. He has appeared in the films Drumline, Love Don't Cost a Thing and Roll Bounce. As a rapper, he released his first album of the same name in 2003 with the single "Gigolo", in collaboration with singer R. Kelly. In 2007, he played the fictional footballer TJ Harper in the movie Goal II: Living in a dream. In 2006, Cannon recorded the singles "Dime Piece" and "My Wife" for the planned album Stages, which was never released. Cannon married American singer Mariah Carey in 2008. The couple separated and filed for divorce in December 2014. The divorce ended in 2016 and they are raising their children together.
