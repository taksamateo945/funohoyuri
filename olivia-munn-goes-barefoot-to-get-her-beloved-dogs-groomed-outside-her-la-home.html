Olivia Munn goes barefoot to get her beloved dogs groomed outside her LA home

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Tuesday, June 11, 2024

She’s a well-known animal advocate.

Olivia Munn, 40, takes great care of her furry friends and was spotted getting her rescue dogs groomed at a mobile groomer outside of her Los Angeles home on Monday morning.

The actress recently found herself in the headlines after being romantically linked to comic John Mulaney following his recent split from estranged wife Anna Marie Tendler.

Munn didn’t even bother to throw her shoes on while shuttling her two dogs to and fro the mobile grooming van parked outside of her house.

The bombshell was dressed down in leggings and an oversized plaid shirt with a face mask and eye glasses.

She affectionately carried her rescue pups, Chance and Frankie, from the groomer after they’d had their canine spa day. 

Olivia is rumored to be dating Saturday Night Live writer and stand-up comedian John Mulaney, according to a report from People.
