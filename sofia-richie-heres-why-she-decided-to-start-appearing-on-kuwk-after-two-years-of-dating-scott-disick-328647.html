Sofia Richie Heres Why She Decided To Start Appearing On KUWK After Two Years Of Dating Scott

Posted by Martina Birk on Saturday, April 6, 2024

Sofia Richie and Scott Disick have been in a relationship for a couple of years but the young model is just now starting to appear on Keeping Up with the Kardashians. She could have been part of the show much longer ago but it’s taken her a while to decide it’s something she wants.

Here’s what determined her to join the reality TV series!

One source close to the model told HollywoodLife that she did not want to be pressured into it and instead, she wanted all her cameos on KUWK to be her decision.

As fans know, Sofia appeared on the family series for the first time on the October 6 episode, which featured a trip to Finland alongside Scott Disick and his ex, Kourtney Kardashian.

The insider explains that ‘Sophia was very hesitant to shoot the show, but the whole family really encouraged her to overcome her shyness and just do it. And, she’s very happy she did.’

Apparently, part of the reason she is so glad she took this decision is that it’s managed to strengthen her and Scott’s relationship even more.

After all, ‘The show is a huge part of Scott’s life and if they’re going to be together it just makes sense for her to take part — so that’s really the main factor in her deciding to do this. And, it’s great for her brand. She’s ambitious, so why not take things to the next level?’

Of course, Sofia is not a permanent member of the cast and she is also not planning to become one.
