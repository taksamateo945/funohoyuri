Obituary: Stephen J. Zelusky

It is with great sadness that we announce the passing of Stephen J. Zelusky, a beloved member of the Deer Lake community. Stephen was born on November 24, 1963, in Pottsville, Pennsylvania, to Verna and Francis Zelusky. He was a devoted husband to Rosanne Zelusky and a loving father to his children, Stephen F., Sara, Marshall, and Mesa. Stephen’s passion for music was evident throughout his life, as he founded and led the renowned rock band Sapphire, captivating audiences in Schuylkill County and beyond.

Early Life and Education

Stephen’s journey began in Pottsville, where he grew up and attended Minersville Area High School. After graduating in 1981, he pursued his passion for electrical engineering technology at Penn State University, earning an associate’s degree. His dedication and expertise in this field led him to a successful career as an Automation Specialist at Shingle and Gibb Automation in New Jersey.

Community Involvement

Stephen was deeply committed to his community and actively involved in various organizations. He was a proud member of St. Ambrose Roman Catholic Church in Schuylkill Haven, where he contributed to the Men of St. Ambrose. Additionally, he served as a valued member of the Liberty Fire Co. and the Second Mountain Gun Club. Stephen’s dedication to his community extended beyond his professional and social affiliations. He was known for his willingness to lend a helping hand and his genuine care for others.

Stephen’s legacy will forever be remembered by his family, friends, and all those whose lives he touched. His warm smile, passion for music, and unwavering commitment to his loved ones will be cherished and celebrated. As we mourn his loss, let us also remember the joy and love he brought into our lives.

Passions and Achievements

Stephen J. Zelusky was a remarkable individual who pursued his passions with unwavering dedication and achieved great success in various aspects of his life. His remarkable journey was marked by his love for music, his accomplishments as a musician, and his deep devotion to his family.

Music and Band

Stephen’s love for music was a driving force in his life. He not only had a deep appreciation for music but also had the talent and passion to create it. He founded and led Sapphire, a renowned rock band in Schuylkill County. With his brother and close friends, Stephen created captivating melodies and electrifying performances that left audiences in awe. His ability to connect with people through music was truly extraordinary.

Family and Survivors

Stephen’s greatest source of joy and inspiration was his family. He was a devoted husband to Rosanne Zelusky and a loving father to his children, Stephen F., Sara, Marshall, and Mesa. His role as a father and a grandfather brought him immense happiness and fulfillment. Stephen’s legacy lives on through his children and grandson, Owen Zelusky. He was also survived by his brother, Paul Zelusky, and his brothers-in-law, James McCabe and Dennis McCabe. Stephen’s love and support for his family were unwavering, and his memory will forever be cherished by those he leaves behind.

Stephen J. Zelusky’s life was a testament to the power of pursuing one’s passions and cherishing the bonds of family. His musical talent and dedication to his loved ones will continue to inspire and resonate with all who had the privilege of knowing him.
